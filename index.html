Week 2 blog

We spent the second week developing on our ideas using diagrams and wireframes. This is so that we can find holes in our logic and find the problems in the system early on instead of having to cancel features because it causes interference with other features. We have also been talking about which OCBC online banking application to base our application off of to give us the best chance of developing an excellent app.
